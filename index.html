About NastyCanada

NastyCanada was born from a moment that united Canadians in pride and humor. When our neighbors to the south suggested Canada might become the 51st state, we responded with our signature polite defiance: "Canada's Too Nasty to Be 51st."

What started as a viral t-shirt design has grown into a full collection of Canadian-themed apparel and accessories. Our brand embodies the spirit of Canadian independence, resilience, and that unique blend of politeness and attitude that makes Canada, well, nasty in all the right ways.

Every product in our collection - from hoodies and tees to hats and phone cases - is designed with quality, comfort, and Canadian pride in mind. Wear your NastyCanada gear with confidence, knowing you're representing the great white north with style.

Shipping Information

We ship to both Canada and the United States. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within Canada and 7-10 business days to the US. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn and in original packaging. Contact us for a return authorization before shipping items back.

Product Quality

All NastyCanada products are made with premium materials and designed to last. Our apparel features durable printing that won't fade or crack with proper care.

Uploading Your Product Photos

  1. Navigate to the /public/images/products/ folder in your Vercel project
  2. Upload your product images to this folder
  3. Name your images using these exact filenames:
    • hoodie.jpg - For your NastyCanada hoodie
    • tshirt.jpg - For your NastyCanada t-shirt
    • snapback.jpg - For your NastyCanada hat
    • phone-case.jpg - For your iPhone case
    • collection-banner.jpg - For the email signup background
  4. The website will automatically use your new images once uploaded
